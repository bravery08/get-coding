Stolen Diamond Discovered in Siberia!

Professor Bairstone and Dr. Day have made a sensational discovery! They have discovered the Monk Diamond in a remote cave in Siberia. Professor Bairstone's dog, Ernest, sniffed out the diamond.

The jewel was stolen 3 years ago from the House of Volkov. The prime suspects in the theft were the Bond Brothers. The team thinks the diamond had been hidden by the thieves.
